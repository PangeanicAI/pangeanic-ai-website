## Overview

Speech datasets are foundational for AI systems that must understand human communication across languages, accents, dialects, acoustic environments and conversational conditions.

Modern ASR, conversational AI and multimodal systems require more than clean studio audio. Production-grade AI systems must process spontaneous speech, overlapping speakers, noisy environments, code-switching behavior and multilingual interaction patterns operating under real-world conditions.

This repository documents Pangeanic’s speech dataset capabilities for multilingual AI systems, conversational AI, speech intelligence, ASR, TTS, AI copilots and enterprise audio workflows.

Pangeanic structures speech datasets as operational AI assets designed around realistic deployment environments, multilingual communication patterns and enterprise-grade governance requirements.
