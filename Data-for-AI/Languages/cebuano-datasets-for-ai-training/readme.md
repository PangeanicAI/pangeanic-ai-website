## Overview

Cebuano AI systems require more than generic multilingual corpora. Enterprise-grade AI applications operating across the Philippines and Southeast Asia must understand conversational Cebuano, regional dialect diversity, English-Cebuano code-switching behavior, culturally grounded terminology and real-world communication patterns.

This repository documents Pangeanic’s Cebuano AI data capabilities for multilingual AI systems, Southeast Asian AI initiatives, speech technologies, machine translation, enterprise NLP and multimodal AI applications.
