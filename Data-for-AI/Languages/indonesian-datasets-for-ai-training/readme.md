## Overview

Indonesian AI systems require more than generic multilingual corpora. Production-ready AI operating across Southeast Asia must understand dialect diversity, regional communication styles, code-switching behavior, culturally grounded terminology and real-world enterprise language usage.

This repository documents Pangeanic’s Indonesian AI data capabilities for multilingual AI systems, Southeast Asian AI initiatives, speech technologies, machine translation, enterprise NLP and multimodal AI applications.
