## Overview

Hmong AI systems require more than generic multilingual corpora. Enterprise-grade AI applications operating across Southeast Asia and multilingual low-resource AI ecosystems must understand tonal speech behavior, conversational Hmong, dialect diversity, culturally grounded terminology and real-world communication patterns.

This repository documents Pangeanic’s Hmong AI data capabilities for multilingual AI systems, Southeast Asian AI initiatives, speech technologies, machine translation, enterprise NLP and multimodal AI applications.
