## Overview

Nepali AI systems require more than generic multilingual corpora. Enterprise-grade AI applications operating across Nepal and South Asian multilingual ecosystems must understand conversational Nepali, regional dialect diversity, Nepali-English code-switching behavior, culturally grounded terminology and real-world enterprise communication patterns.

This repository documents Pangeanic’s Nepali AI data capabilities for multilingual AI systems, South Asian AI initiatives, speech technologies, machine translation, enterprise NLP and multimodal AI applications.
