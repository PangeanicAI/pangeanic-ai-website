## Overview

Urdu AI systems require more than generic multilingual corpora. Enterprise-grade AI applications operating across Pakistan, India and South Asian multilingual ecosystems must understand conversational Urdu, regional dialect diversity, Urdu-English code-switching behavior, culturally grounded terminology and real-world enterprise communication patterns.

This repository documents Pangeanic’s Urdu AI data capabilities for multilingual AI systems, South Asian AI initiatives, speech technologies, machine translation, enterprise NLP and multimodal AI applications.
