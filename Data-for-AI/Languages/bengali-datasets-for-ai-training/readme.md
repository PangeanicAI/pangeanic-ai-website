## Overview

Bengali AI systems require more than generic multilingual corpora. Enterprise-grade AI applications operating across Bangladesh, India and South Asian multilingual ecosystems must understand conversational Bengali, regional dialect diversity, Bengali-English code-switching behavior, culturally grounded terminology and real-world enterprise communication patterns.

This repository documents Pangeanic’s Bengali AI data capabilities for multilingual AI systems, South Asian AI initiatives, speech technologies, machine translation, enterprise NLP and multimodal AI applications.
