# Kurdish Datasets for Multilingual LLMs, ASR and Sovereign AI

Enterprise-grade Kurdish datasets for AI training, multilingual LLM fine-tuning, ASR, OCR, conversational AI and multimodal systems across Kurdish language ecosystems.

## Overview

Kurdish AI systems require more than generic multilingual corpora. Production-ready AI operating across Kurdish-speaking regions must understand dialect diversity, code-switching behavior, regional communication patterns, multilingual interaction and low-resource linguistic environments.

This repository documents Pangeanic’s Kurdish AI data capabilities for multilingual AI systems, sovereign AI initiatives, speech technologies, machine translation, enterprise NLP and multimodal AI applications.
