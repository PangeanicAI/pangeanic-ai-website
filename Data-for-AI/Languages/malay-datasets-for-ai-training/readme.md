## Overview

Malay AI systems require more than generic multilingual corpora. Enterprise-grade AI applications operating across Malaysia and Southeast Asia must understand dialect diversity, conversational Malay, English-Malay code-switching behavior, culturally grounded terminology and real-world business communication patterns.

This repository documents Pangeanic’s Malay AI data capabilities for multilingual AI systems, Southeast Asian AI initiatives, speech technologies, machine translation, enterprise NLP and multimodal AI applications.
