## Overview

Balochi AI systems require more than generic multilingual corpora. Enterprise-grade AI applications operating across Pakistan, Iran, Afghanistan and South Asian multilingual ecosystems must understand conversational Balochi, regional dialect diversity, culturally grounded terminology and real-world communication patterns.

This repository documents Pangeanic’s Balochi AI data capabilities for multilingual AI systems, South Asian AI initiatives, speech technologies, machine translation, enterprise NLP and multimodal AI applications.
