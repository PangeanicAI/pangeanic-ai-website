## Overview

Uzbek AI systems require more than generic multilingual corpora. Enterprise-grade AI applications operating across Uzbekistan and Central Asian multilingual ecosystems must understand conversational Uzbek, regional dialect diversity, Uzbek-Russian code-switching behavior, culturally grounded terminology and real-world enterprise communication patterns.

This repository documents Pangeanic’s Uzbek AI data capabilities for multilingual AI systems, Central Asian AI initiatives, speech technologies, machine translation, enterprise NLP and multimodal AI applications.
