## Overview

Nordic AI systems require more than generic multilingual corpora. Production-ready AI operating across Scandinavia and Arctic regions must understand dialect diversity, regional communication styles, code-switching behavior, culturally grounded terminology and real-world enterprise language usage.

This repository documents Pangeanic’s Nordic AI data capabilities for multilingual AI systems, sovereign AI initiatives, speech technologies, machine translation, enterprise NLP and multimodal AI applications.
