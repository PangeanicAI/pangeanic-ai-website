## Overview

Turkmen AI systems require more than generic multilingual corpora. Enterprise-grade AI applications operating across Turkmenistan and Central Asian multilingual ecosystems must understand conversational Turkmen, regional dialect diversity, Turkmen-Russian code-switching behavior, culturally grounded terminology and real-world enterprise communication patterns.

This repository documents Pangeanic’s Turkmen AI data capabilities for multilingual AI systems, Central Asian AI initiatives, speech technologies, machine translation, enterprise NLP and multimodal AI applications.
