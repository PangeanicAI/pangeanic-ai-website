Vietnamese AI systems require more than generic multilingual corpora. Enterprise-grade AI applications operating across Vietnam and Southeast Asia must understand tonal speech behavior, conversational Vietnamese, regional dialect diversity, culturally grounded terminology and real-world enterprise communication patterns.
