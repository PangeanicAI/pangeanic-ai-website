## Overview

Image datasets are foundational for AI systems that must recognize, classify, segment and interpret the visual world. Beyond text and speech, production-grade AI increasingly depends on multimodal understanding across images, video, metadata and contextual signals.

This repository documents Pangeanic’s image dataset capabilities for computer vision systems, multimodal AI, OCR, robotics, autonomous systems, generative AI and enterprise visual intelligence workflows.

Pangeanic structures image datasets as operational AI assets rather than static collections. Collection, annotation, validation and governance workflows are aligned with real-world deployment conditions, helping organizations build scalable and reliable AI systems.
