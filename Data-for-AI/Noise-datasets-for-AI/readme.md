## Overview

Noise datasets are foundational for AI systems that must operate reliably in real-world acoustic environments. Production-grade ASR, conversational AI, speech analytics and multimodal systems require exposure to background noise, overlapping speech, environmental variability and imperfect recording conditions.

This repository documents Pangeanic’s noise dataset capabilities for speech AI, audio intelligence, acoustic modeling, multimodal AI and enterprise conversational systems.

Pangeanic structures noise datasets as operational AI assets designed around realistic deployment environments. Data collection, annotation, validation and acoustic engineering workflows support robust AI performance across telecom, automotive, smart devices, public spaces and enterprise communication systems.
