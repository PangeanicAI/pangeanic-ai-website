## Overview

Modern AI systems depend on far more than raw data volume. Enterprise AI increasingly requires multilingual coverage, metadata governance, human validation, domain adaptation, annotation consistency and deployment-oriented data engineering.

Pangeanic provides multilingual AI training datasets and governed AI data operations across language, speech, image, video, OCR, conversational AI, instruction tuning, evaluation and model alignment workflows.

This repository documents Pangeanic’s Data-for-AI capabilities for multilingual LLMs, ASR, OCR, computer vision, conversational AI, sovereign AI systems and enterprise AI deployment pipelines.

Pangeanic structures AI datasets as operational infrastructure rather than static data assets. Collection, validation, alignment and governance workflows are designed around real-world enterprise AI environments requiring scalability, compliance and multilingual precision.
